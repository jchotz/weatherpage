🌍 What is WeatherPage.info?

WeatherPage.info gives you a dedicated, mobile-friendly weather page for up to 10 locations. See current conditions plus the past 10 days of weather history — all in one simple link you can save to your phone's home screen. No app to download, no account required.

  • ✓ Up to 10 saved locations
  • ✓ Current conditions at a glance
  • ✓ 10-day historical weather
  • ✓ Mobile, tablet & desktop friendly
  • ✓ Save link to home screen
  • ✓ No app download needed

📱 How to Add to Your Home Screen

1
Open Safari on your iPhone or iPad and go to weatherpage.info
2
Tap the Share button (the box with an arrow) at the bottom of the screen
3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4
Tap Add — it now lives on your home screen like an app
