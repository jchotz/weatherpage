Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 22, 2026

🔒 The short version: We collect only what's needed to create your weather page. We never share, sell, or distribute your information — ever.

Who We Are

WeatherPage.info is a simple service that creates a personal, mobile-friendly weather page for any location you choose. No account is required. No tracking. No ads.

Information We Collect

When you use the "Get Started Now" form, we collect:

  • Your city and state
  • Your USA phone number

This information is used solely to generate your personal weather page and, in the future, to send you weather-related notifications you explicitly request.

How We Use Your Information

  • To look up geographic coordinates for your city
  • To generate a personalized weather page at a unique URL
  • To send you your page link via SMS (when enabled)

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose beyond the above.

We Never Share Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, lease, or otherwise share your personal information with any third party — ever. This includes:

  • Advertisers or marketing companies
  • Data brokers
  • Other websites or services
  • Government agencies (except as required by law)

Third-Party Services

WeatherPage.info uses the following third-party services to function. Your data is not shared with them beyond what is technically necessary:

  • Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) — provides weather forecast and history data using your city's latitude and longitude. No personal information is transmitted.
  • Twilio — used to deliver SMS notifications. Your phone number is transmitted to Twilio only to send you your page link, and only if you choose to receive it.
  • Google Fonts — used to load the Inter typeface. Standard font requests may include your IP address per Google's own privacy policy.

Data Storage

Your city, state, and phone number are stored in a simple file on our server for the sole purpose of maintaining your weather page. We do not store payment information, passwords, or sensitive personal data of any kind.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your weather page is active. You may request deletion at any time by using the "Remove Your Phone Number" option on the home page or by contacting us directly.

Children's Privacy

WeatherPage.info is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We will never make changes that reduce your privacy protections without clear notice.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at weatherpage.info.
